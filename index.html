Caitlin Ironside is a graphic designer based in New York City.

Working across a variety of mediums, she aims to create outcomes which possess intelligence, beauty, relevance and simplicity.

Currently, she is most interested in how the tactility of the print medium can sit happily inside digital, interactive spaces and vice versa. She is exploring creative code to this end (and really enjoying herself).
